About

A small, slow gathering — by design.

We started Awakened in 2022 because every wellness summit we'd been to felt like a funnel with a healing veneer — twenty back-to-back sales pitches in a Zoom skin.

This is the opposite. Twenty-four practitioners, one hour at a time, live and unrecorded. You show up, take notes, and integrate before the next conversation begins. That's the whole product.

If you've ever wished a summit didn't try to sell you something every fourteen minutes — this is for you.

Common questions

FAQ

Will sessions be recorded?

No — live only. The intimacy of an unrecorded room is what makes practitioners share things they wouldn't otherwise.

Is there a paid track or VIP tier?

No upsells, no premium pass. Donations open after the closing call if you want to keep us going.

How long is each session?

Fifty minutes of conversation, twenty minutes of live Q&A.

Where does it run?

Zoom — link arrives in your inbox the morning of, with a one-tap calendar add.

Do I have to attend every session?

No. Most people show up to four or five. Reserve and pick what calls.

Will you sell my email?

No — three emails total, then never again unless you ask.
