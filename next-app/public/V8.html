What this isn't, then what it is.

This is not a motivational event. No one will tell you your child is a gift; you already know. No one will perform calm for you. There are no before-and-after reels.

This is five structured evenings of specific tools, specific scripts, and specific outcomes — indexed, paginated, and tested against 73,124 households. Live 7:00 to 9:30 PM Eastern, Tuesday through Saturday. Replays within 48 hours.

You will leave with one sentence you weren't saying, one three-minute morning block that actually holds, and one clinician's name for the conversation you've been postponing.
