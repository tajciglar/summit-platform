The week in five sentences

One line you won't say again.

  1. I.

    "You're overreacting" — replaced with "Your body looks like it's had too much. Let's find a smaller room."

    Mon · Day 01 takeaway

  2. II.

    "Why do I have to say it four times?" — replaced with two silent cards on the counter.

    Tue · Day 02 takeaway

  3. III.

    "Put your shoes on" — replaced with a transition anchor your kid has actually agreed to use.

    Wed · Day 03 takeaway

  4. IV.

    "She's so sensitive" — replaced with an actual name for the feeling, so she can name it herself next time.

    Thu · Day 04 takeaway

  5. V.

    "We'll just figure it out with the school" — replaced with a printed, dated accommodation request and the exact paragraph to email.

    Fri · Day 05 takeaway
