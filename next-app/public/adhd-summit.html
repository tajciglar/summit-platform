What this actually is.

  1. 01
    Evidence-led

    Every session pins to a clinician or a study.

    Barkley, Dodson, Shanker, Siegel — the people the field cites. No "science-backed" over Pinterest quotes. 26 of the 41 speakers hold a clinical PhD or MD.

  2. 02
    Specific, not inspirational

    Scripts for the house you already live in.

    Not "imagine a calm morning." The actual sentence to say when the shoe card is still on the counter and the bus leaves in 11 minutes.

  3. 03
    Free, and actually free

    Live attendance plus 48-hour replays, no card required.

    One optional lifetime-access pass after registration ($47). If you skip it, nothing breaks. No mid-session upsells — that's a rule we keep.
