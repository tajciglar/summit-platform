When is the summit happening?
The ADHD Parenting Summit runs Tuesday 10 February through Saturday 14 February 2026. Each evening’s sessions begin at 7:00 PM ET and close at 9:30 PM ET.
Is the summit available in my country?
Yes. It’s a free online event accessible from anywhere in the world. If you can’t watch everything live, you can upgrade to a VIP pass for lifetime access.
How can I watch the recordings?
You’ll receive links two days before the summit begins. Sessions unlock automatically and are free to watch for 48 hours after each evening.
I’m busy during the summit. Can I still watch the videos?
Each evening’s recordings are available for 48 hours. For unlimited lifetime access, upgrade to the VIP pass.
Is the summit a live event?
Sessions are pre-recorded and released together each evening; replays stay live for 48 hours. Saturday closes with a live Q&A.
How many speakers are featured each day?
Each evening features 6 speakers across 150 minutes, with 25–45 minute sessions.

All sessions are pre-recorded. During the 48-hour viewing window you can watch any speaker from that day at your own pace.

Try clearing your browser cache and cookies, update your browser, or switch devices. If the issue persists, please email hello@althea-academy.com.
I signed up but didn’t receive any links.

Yes — please contact us after the summit and we’ll send you detailed instructions.
