The ADHD Parenting Summit 2026, in plain daylight.

For too long, our children with ADHD have been labeled "lazy," "problematic," and "difficult." It is time to rewrite the parenting playbook — the one that treats brightness like a fault line instead of a map.

Why this is more important

Than ever before.

ADHD is the top mental or developmental condition among children. [1]

Every 3rd child with ADHD has symptoms that last into adulthood. [2]

A child with ADHD has a 1-in-4 chance of having a parent who also has it. [3]

Children with ADHD are two to three times more likely to develop substance abuse or addiction. [4]

Children with ADHD are more likely to develop low self-esteem, anxiety, and depression. [5]

Compared to their peers, children with ADHD earn significantly lower school grades. [6]

But here's the good news.

The earlier you learn how to support your child with ADHD, the better your chances of managing the condition and minimising long-term issues.

Frequently asked questions

FAQ

When is the Summit happening?
The ADHD Parenting Summit runs Tuesday Feb 10 through Saturday Feb 14, 2026. Each day's sessions begin at 9 AM ET / 3 PM CET.
Is the Summit available in my country?
Yes — it's a free online event, accessible from anywhere in the world. If you can't watch everything live, you can upgrade to a VIP Pass for lifetime access.
How can I watch the recordings?
You'll receive links two days before the Summit begins. Sessions unlock automatically at 9 AM ET each day and are free to watch for 24 hours.
I'm busy during the summit — can I still watch?
Each day's recordings are available for 24 hours. For unlimited lifetime access, upgrade to our VIP Pass.
Is the Summit a live event?
No — all sessions are pre-recorded and released at the same time each day. You'll have free access to videos for 24 hours after release.
How many speakers are featured each day?
Each day features 8–9 sessions, with each session lasting 30–60 minutes. All sessions air simultaneously each day.
Where can I find the schedule for each speaker?
All sessions are pre-recorded. During the 24-hour viewing window you can watch any speaker from that day at your own pace.
I'm having technical issues — what should I do?
Try clearing your browser's cache and cookies, update your browser, or try a different device. If the issue persists, please email us.
I signed up but didn't receive any links.
Check all inbox folders (spam, junk, promotions). If still no email, reach out and we'll help.
What is included in the VIP Pass?
Lifetime access to all 40+ masterclasses, Expert Action Blueprints, audio edition, subtitles, and the Action-Taker's Workbook.
Do I get a Certificate of Attendance?
Yes — please contact us and we'll send detailed instructions.
