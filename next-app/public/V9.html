Prelude

A programme, not a performance.

This is five evenings, not forty. No motivational spine, no before-and-after reels, no one telling you your child is a gift — you already know. Performance calm is not the point.

What you get instead is a scored programme: five evenings, four sessions each, timed to the minute. Forty-one contributors speaking directly to parents of ADHD kids ages 5 – 17. Live 7:00 – 9:30 PM Eastern; replays within 48 hours.

You will leave with one sentence you weren't saying, one three-minute morning that holds, and one clinician's name for the conversation you've been postponing.

Notes & queries

Before you register.

Is it really free, or is there a catch?
Yes, free. Live attendance and 48-hour replays are included. A lifetime-access pass is available for $47 after registering — fully optional; no mid-session upsells.
What time are the evenings?
Live 7:00 – 9:30 PM Eastern, Tuesday February 10 through Saturday February 14. Replays stay live for 48 hours after each session.
My child hasn't been diagnosed. Is this still for me?
Yes. About 31% of attendees arrive pre-diagnosis. We cover the clinical landscape and questions to ask your pediatrician — specific decisions stay with you and your prescriber.
Can I watch with my partner?
Yes. One registration covers the whole household. Most attendees watch with a spouse, co-parent, or grandparent at least one evening.
Do you sell or share my email?
No. Your address stays with Althea Academy. No partner lists, no cross-promotions. Unsubscribe link in every message.
