II.

An Unhurried Answer to an Overwhelming Question

For too long, children with ADHD have been labelled lazy, problematic, difficult. We built the ADHD Parenting Summit because we could not find the thing we needed: a generous, evidence-based conversation that treats ADHD parenting as something other than a problem to be fixed. Across five days, forty of the field's most thoughtful clinicians, educators and writers offer practical guidance — and, just as importantly, the reassurance that you are not alone in the doubt.

For our brand-new 2026 edition we are spreading the curriculum across five full days instead of three — a deeper understanding of your child's ADHD, more practical strategies, and more time to absorb it all. Watch live or catch the replays at your own pace. Bring your partner, your mother, your co-parent.

All so you can help your child thrive with ADHD.

    End the Yelling Cycle

    Calm responses that actually work — not because you found willpower, but because you found a different script.

  3. III.

    Navigate the Digital Age

    Raising an ADHD child around screens without a nightly battle. Practical agreements, not prohibitions.

    Regulate Big Emotions

    Help your child regulate big emotions — not suppress them. The difference, over time, changes everything.

    Routines that support brain development and well-being. No charts with gold stars — things that hold up in real kitchens.

Questions, Answered

When is the Summit happening?
The ADHD Parenting Summit runs Tuesday, February 10 through Saturday, February 14, 2026. Each day's sessions unlock at 9 AM ET / 3 PM CEST.
Is it really free?
Yes, entirely. All 40+ sessions, the Mastery Collection, and the three bonus sidebars. You'll receive upgrade offers later — these are optional and never gate the free content.
Is the Summit available in my country?
Yes — it's a free online event, accessible from anywhere in the world. If you can't watch everything live, you can upgrade to a VIP Pass for lifetime access.
How can I watch the recordings?
You'll receive links two days before the Summit begins. Each day's sessions unlock automatically at 9 AM ET and are free to watch for 24 hours.
I'm busy during the summit. Can I still watch the videos?
Each day's recordings are available for 24 hours. For unlimited lifetime access, upgrade to our VIP Pass.
Is this suitable if my child isn't formally diagnosed?
Absolutely. Many of our attendees are observing ADHD traits and deciding whether to pursue assessment. The content is relevant regardless of diagnostic status.
How many speakers are featured each day?
Each day features 6–9 sessions, with each session lasting 30–60 minutes. All sessions air simultaneously each day.
My partner wants to attend too — do they need a separate seat?
One registration covers your household. Bring your partner, grandparents, co-parent, or an older child if appropriate.
I signed up but didn't receive any links.
Check all inbox folders (spam, junk, promotions). If you still can't find the email, please reach out and we'll help.
What is included in the VIP Pass?
Lifetime access to all 40+ masterclasses, Expert Action Blueprints, audio edition, subtitles, and the Action-Taker's Workbook.

Yes — please contact us and we'll send detailed instructions.
