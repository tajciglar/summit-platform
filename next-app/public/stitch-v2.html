Before you register.

Live 7:00 – 9:30 PM Eastern, Tuesday through Saturday. Replays stay available for 48 hours after each evening.
My child hasn’t been diagnosed. Is this still for me?
Yes. About 31% of 2025 attendees arrived pre-diagnosis. We cover the clinical landscape and the questions to ask your prescriber; specific decisions stay with you and your pediatrician.
Can I watch with my partner?
Yes — one registration per household covers the whole home.
What happens to my email?
One calendar email, a reminder the night before each session, and an optional summary afterwards. We do not share or rent addresses.
