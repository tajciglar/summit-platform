A Prospectus

What this week is, and what it is not.

It is

  • Five live evenings of four sessions each, 7:00 – 9:30 PM ET.
  • Clinicians, neuroscientists, and educators — each vetted, cited, and asked to be specific.
  • Replays available within 48 hours of each evening.

It is not

  • A sales funnel disguised as education — no upsells during sessions.
  • A medical consultation. Your pediatrician still has final say.
  • Yet another webinar of rebranded willpower advice.

Questions

Before you register.

Is it really free?
Yes. Live attendance and same-day replays are free. A lifetime-access pass is available for $47 after registering — fully optional.
What time are the sessions?
Live 7:00 – 9:30 PM Eastern, Tuesday through Saturday. Replays remain available for 48 hours after each evening.
My child hasn’t been diagnosed. Is this still for me?
Yes. About 31% of our 2025 attendees arrived pre-diagnosis. We cover the clinical landscape and the questions to ask your prescriber; specific decisions stay with you and your pediatrician.
Can I watch with my partner?
Yes — one registration per household covers the whole home.
What happens to my email?
One calendar email, a reminder the night before each session, and an optional summary afterwards. We don’t share or rent addresses.
