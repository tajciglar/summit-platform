Is this summit really free? ▼
Yes! All live sessions are 100% free to attend. You can watch every presentation during the 5-day event at no cost. We also offer an optional All-Access Pass if you want lifetime replays and bonus materials.
How long are the sessions available to watch? ▼
Each day's sessions are available for 24 hours after they air. This gives you flexibility to watch at a time that suits your family's schedule. The All-Access Pass gives you lifetime access to all recordings.
What if I can't watch all five days? ▼
That's completely fine! Watch what you can, when you can. We'll send you a daily schedule with session highlights so you can prioritize the topics most relevant to your family. Every session has value on its own.
Is this only for parents of diagnosed children? ▼
Not at all. Whether your child has a formal diagnosis, is in the assessment process, or you simply suspect ADHD, these strategies will help. The summit also benefits teachers, grandparents, and anyone involved in raising a child with attention challenges.
Can I ask the speakers questions? ▼
Yes! We host live Q&A sessions after select presentations where you can ask speakers your specific questions directly. Our moderators ensure every parent gets heard. You can also submit questions in advance when you register.
How do I unsubscribe if I change my mind? ▼
