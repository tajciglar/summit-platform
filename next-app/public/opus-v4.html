Common questions

Quick answers

Is it really free? +

Yes, entirely. All 40 sessions, the Collection, and the 3 bonuses. Optional upgrade offers come later — they never gate the free content.

What if I miss a live session? +

Each session is recorded and available for 48 hours after it airs. Registered attendees with the All-Access Pass keep permanent access.

Is this suitable if my child isn't formally diagnosed? +

Absolutely. Many attendees are observing ADHD traits and deciding whether to pursue assessment. Content is relevant regardless.

My partner wants to attend — separate seat? +

One registration covers your household. Bring your partner, grandparents, co-parent, or an older child if appropriate.

Is medical advice offered? +

Contributors are qualified clinicians, but the summit is educational, not clinical. We'll point you toward where to go for personalized medical guidance.
