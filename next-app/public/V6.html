Frequently asked

Before you register.

When is the Summit happening?
The ADHD Parenting Summit runs Monday, March 9 through Friday, March 13, 2026. Each day’s sessions begin at 9 AM ET / 3 PM CEST.
Is the Summit available in my country?
Yes. It’s a free online event accessible from anywhere in the world. If you can’t watch everything live, you can upgrade to a VIP Pass for lifetime access.
How can I watch the recordings?
You’ll receive links two days before the Summit begins. Sessions unlock automatically at 9 AM ET each day and are free to watch for 24 hours.
I’m busy during the Summit — can I still watch?
Each day’s recordings are free for 24 hours after they go live. For unlimited lifetime access, upgrade to the VIP Pass.
Is the Summit a live event?
All sessions are pre-recorded and released at the same time each day. You’ll have free access to videos for 24 hours after release.
How many speakers per day?
Each day features 8–9 sessions; each session is 30–60 minutes. All sessions air simultaneously each day.
Where do I find each speaker’s schedule?
All sessions are pre-recorded. During each 24-hour viewing window you can watch any speaker from that day at your own pace.
My child hasn’t been diagnosed — is this still for me?
Absolutely. About 31% of attendees arrive pre-diagnosis. Day 02 directly addresses “should we pursue an evaluation?”
Is any of this medication advice?
No. We cover the clinical landscape and questions to ask your prescriber — specific decisions remain with you and your pediatrician.
I’m having technical issues — what should I do?
Try clearing your browser cache and cookies, update your browser, or try a different device. If the issue persists, email us and we’ll sort it.
I signed up but didn’t receive any links.
Check all inbox folders (spam, junk, promotions). If still nothing, reach out and we’ll help you get access.
What’s included in the VIP Pass?
Lifetime access to all 40+ masterclasses, Expert Action Blueprints, the audio edition, subtitles, and the Action-Taker’s Workbook.
Can I watch with my partner?
Yes — one registration per household covers the whole home.
Do I get a Certificate of Attendance?
Yes. Contact us after attending and we’ll send detailed instructions.
